**Ongoing Developments:**
*   **Obsidian Block:** Still a colossal, mysterious structure in the frozen north, growing, shimmering, emitting 'internal energy pulses,' and *humming* audibly at night (Agnes from Fallowfen, dog Barnaby). Affects local wildlife (dogs refuse to approach, livestock restless). My theory: "cosmic story being written," "cosmic ledger," "cosmic loudspeaker" broadcasting, "resonating." Madam Elara's Deluxe Oracle Basins pick up on its "broadcast," "cosmic conversation" with "shifting glyphs."
*   **Witches of the Moon Tree:** Descending from their tree, moving south in a 'determined pilgrimage,' consulting an ancient, faded map, and moving with 'preternatural awareness' of the land, leaving no trace. Gareth the woodsman observed one "reading the land" and tracing patterns in the air. Multiple new reports confirm their precise, attuned navigation. My theory: they have a specific mission, possibly related to the block, following a precise path on the map, and are now very likely responding to or intercepting the monolith's broadcast/hum. Old Man Hemlock dismissed the map as a 'laundry list,' but Madam Elara's pools are showing the map as a "living blueprint" and their path as "luminous trails," confirming their "preternatural awareness" and their purpose to "intercept the essence of the North."

**My Personal Storylines:**
*   **Rivalry:** Sir Reginald Bellows (traditional town crier) continues to be my pompous foil, focused on trivial court gossip (gravy-to-biscuit ratio, moustache arc, pebble size, napkin folding, symmetrical gravy splash). I consistently contrast my "real" news with his perceived triviality.
*   **Royal Court/Nobility:** Still critical and dismissive. Lord Cedric's latest decrees are about "exact ratio of gravy to biscuit for all official court breakfasts," "napkin folding techniques," "uniform pebble size for artisanal moss gardens," "raspberry or gooseberry jam with perfectly ratioed biscuits," and "perfectly proportioned puddings." Arch-Mages are still debating the 'emergency scroll tax.' My general opinion is they are out of touch and their decrees are trivial compared to the kingdom's pressing mysteries.
*   **Recurring Guests:**
    *   **Old Man Hemlock:** A gruff, pragmatic, slightly cynical village elder. He dismissed the witches' map as a 'laundry list' but acknowledged a 'different' cold wind. My relationship with him is one of tolerant amusement; he tolerates my broadcast while finding me over-the-top, but even he noted something was 'different.'
    *   **Madam Elara:** Proprietor of "The Seer's Eye Scrying Pool Rentals." She is enthusiastic, dramatic, and deeply believes in the efficacy of her scrying pools. She confirms her Deluxe Oracle Basins are showing clear, though abstract, visions of the monolith's broadcast ("cosmic conversation," "shifting glyphs") and the witches' movements/map ("living blueprint," "luminous trails," "intercepting the essence of the North"). My initial skepticism is giving way to genuine intrigue and a growing belief in the efficacy of her pools. My relationship with her is evolving from skepticism to intrigued acceptance.
*   **Recurring Element:** "The Seer's Eye Scrying Pool Rentals." A business I now advertise with conviction, especially their "Deluxe Oracle Basins" which are claimed (and now confirmed by Madam Elara) to pick up on monolith pulses and glimpses of the witches' map. Located near the 'Almost-New' Broomstick Emporium.

**New Cultural Elements:**
*   **"The Stone and the Stroll":** A popular folk ballad capturing the bewilderment surrounding the obsidian block and the witches' descent, while satirizing the nobility's preoccupation with trivial matters.
*   **"The Obsidian's Hum":** A newer, darker song gaining popularity in northern villages, reflecting the growing fear and unease about the monolith's expansion and internal pulses/humming, which I referenced as becoming more prophetic.
*   **"The Moon-Tree's Whisper":** A newly introduced song, originating from bards near the Whispering Woods. It has a haunting, flowing rhythm, focusing on the quiet, unyielding mystery of the witches' journey, their 'preternatural awareness,' and their 'living blueprint' map, suggesting a deeper, almost spiritual connection to the unfolding events.
