**Ongoing Developments:**
*   **Obsidian Block (North):** Colossal, hundred yards tall, miles across, covered in runes. Appeared with no explanation. Its influence is spreading, causing light objects (hats, awnings, feather boas, socks) to experience brief, inexplicable lift, creating a distinct 'airy looseness'. Roddy theorizes it's actively messing with reality, a "beard-defying, fish-craving magnet" and a "cosmic canvas painter," amplifying subtle currents. Cravings for pickled herring in expedition teams, and glazed river pebbles in guardsmen and badgers. Painting the northern sky with silent, shifting rivers of deep indigo and shimmering emerald. Barnaby's cart, carrying his failed 'Brine & Beards Emporium', was last seen floating downriver, emitting off-key shanties.
*   **Moon Tree Witches:** Descending from the Moon Tree for the first time in living memory, for unknown reasons. Still performing 'Harmonic Attunement' at the Sky-Spire's base. Generating shared dreams and a 'Verdant Imbuement' (Professor Quill calls it 'Sympathetic Flora-Sentience' and 'botanical-performative exuberance'). Scarecrows are dancing/humming, pumpkins pulsing/composing haikus, sunflowers doing interpretive dances, gourds composing haikus, and sunflowers performing synchronized interpretive dances. Royal Rose Garden flora has become politically active: 'Rosa the Revolutionary' (a rose bush) is demanding representation on the Royal Garden Committee and 'pollinator rights' legislation; Grand Duke's petunias are forming a floral picket line, chanting 'equal sunlight for all saplings!'. Professor Quill describes this as a "collective, verdant consciousness" and a "kingdom-wide artistic awakening," a benign communication preparing the land for a grander revelation.
