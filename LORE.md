**Ongoing Developments:**
*   **Obsidian Block (North):** Colossal, hundred yards tall, miles across, covered in runes. Its influence is spreading further south and intensifying, acting as a "cosmic canvas painter," amplifying subtle currents, and subtly *rearranging* the kingdom's fundamental appetites, physics, *animal behavior*, *animal intellect*, and *artistic expression*. Light objects experience 'airy looseness'. Cravings for pickled herring (expedition teams, Thistlewick) and glazed river pebbles (guardsmen, badgers, Royal Stablehands) persist. Northern sky painted with silent, shifting rivers of deep indigo and shimmering emerald.
    *   **Effects:** Farmer Giles's prize-winning pig, Penelope, collects and arranges smooth, flat stones into elaborate geometric patterns. Farmer Giles's chickens cluck in perfectly synchronized, complex rhythms whenever the moon is full. Oat gruel attempts to explain philosophical implications of its own viscosity. Elara's sheep (Whisperwind Downs) are now selectively grazing glow-in-the-dark moss, arranging themselves into constellations on hillsides at dusk, and bleating out names of ancient, forgotten heroes in unison (ovine astronomers/bards).
    *   **Roddy's Theory:** The block is a muse, inspiring artistic, intellectual, and historical memory in the animal kingdom.
*   **Moon Tree Witches:** Descending from the Moon Tree for the first time in living memory. Still performing 'Harmonic Attunement' at the Sky-Spire's base. Generating shared dreams and a 'Verdant Imbuement' ('Sympathetic Flora-Sentience' and 'botanical-performative exuberance').
    *   **Old Man Tiberius's New Dream Report:** Shared dreams of the Moon Tree glowing with a *cold, internal light*, and the witches performing a *silent, intricate dance with wisps of starlight* before unanimously *pointing towards the far north*.
    *   **Effects:** Scarecrows dancing/humming, pumpkins pulsing/composing haikus, sunflowers doing interpretive dances, gourds composing haikus. Royal Rose Garden flora has become increasingly politically active ('Rosa the Revolutionary' demanding 'Bill of Botanical Rights', considering 'ethically sourced compost'). Grand Duke's petunias are forming a floral picket line and have started a 'seed strike'. Militant mint and marigold demanding specific conditions (Dewdrop Distribution Accord brokered with mint). Herbs in the Royal Kitchen garden are refusing to be picked; 'Reginald' the rosemary bush declared a 'culinary strike' demanding 'intellectual property rights' over his flavor profile. 'Parsley the Prophet' whispers recipes for 'revolution stew' to scullery maids. Basil threatens to declare itself a sovereign spice nation.
    *   **Roddy's Theory:** The witches' pointing north might be a warning, an acknowledgement of shared purpose, or a convergence with the obsidian block.

**Roddy's World:**
*   **Rivalry:** **Mortimer Grumbleson, 'The Grand Crier' from 'Herald's Hollow'.** Gravel-voiced, dismissive of the weirdness, offers 'rational explanations'. Roddy considers him a 'dullard' and his journalism 'hollow,' actively advises listeners against his channel and mocks his lack of understanding of Brackenwick's reality, explicitly contrasting his advice with Blythe's and Grizelda's. Roddy jokingly expects Grumbleson's wig to float and predicts he'd dismiss Elara's ovine astronomers as 'bad wool' and Tiberius's dream as 'too much pickled herring.'
*   **Royal Court & Nobility:** Stuffy, well-meaning, and behind the curve.
    *   **Lord Thornweather (Royal Horticulturalist):** Ineffectual, bewildered by politically active flora, last seen attempting to draft a compromise with a militant marigold, looking quite wilted. Still pondering deeply in sighs. Completely overwhelmed by escalating botanical and culinary crises.
    *   **Dame Agnes (Head Gardener, Royal Rose Gardens):** Exasperated by politically active flora, particularly 'Rosa the Revolutionary's' escalated demands. At her wits' end, nearly fainted over Reginald the Rosemary's strike and the threat of herb unionization.
    *   **Grand Duke:** Has demanding petunias on strike and engaged in a 'seed strike'.
    *   **Guardsmen & Royal Stablehands:** Developing cravings for glazed river pebbles.
*   **Recurring Guests:**
    *   **Grizelda (Glint-Gone Goo Emporium):** Alchemist, genius, provides practical solutions. Roddy trusts her. Selling "Gravity-Grip Mist" to counter the 'airy looseness' phenomenon.
    *   **Professor Thessaly Quill (Royal Arch-Librarian & Curator of Whimsical Ephemera):** Scholar, precise, dry wit, knowledgeable. Sees the 'Verdant Imbuement' as a "kingdom-wide artistic awakening" and "benign communication" preparing for a "grander revelation." Her direct opinion on the witches pointing north to be explored. Roddy respects her.
    *   **Bardess Blythe (Flora's Fanfare & Phytopoetry Interpretations):** Sponsor. Seasoned minstrel, botanical diplomat. Offers expanded services for understanding, showcasing, and *mediating with* sentient, politically active greenery. Brokered a provisional 'Dewdrop Distribution Accord' with militant mint. Convinced 'Rosa the Revolutionary' to consider 'ethically sourced compost'. Composed "The Ballad of the Botanic Barricade" and 'Flavorful Mediation Melodies'. Believes the kingdom is undergoing a profound 'evolution'. Roddy and Blythe share a relationship of mutual professional respect and camaraderie.
