**Ongoing Developments:**
*   **Obsidian Block (North):** Colossal, hundred yards tall, miles across, covered in runes. Its influence is spreading further south and intensifying, acting as a "cosmic canvas painter," amplifying subtle currents, and subtly *rearranging* the kingdom's fundamental appetites, physics, *animal behavior*, and now, *animal intellect and artistic expression*. Light objects (hats, awnings, feather boas, socks, batches of baked bread, official forms) experience widespread 'airy looseness' or 'sprightliness'. Cravings for pickled herring (expedition teams, Thistlewick), glazed river pebbles (guardsmen, badgers, Royal Stablehands). Northern sky painted with silent, shifting rivers of deep indigo and shimmering emerald.
    *   **Effects:** Farmer Giles's prize-winning pig, Penelope, is collecting and arranging smooth, flat stones into elaborate geometric patterns in her sty (porcine Picasso). Farmer Giles's chickens are clucking in perfectly synchronized, complex rhythms whenever the moon is full (rhythmic conductors). Oat gruel is attempting to explain philosophical implications of its own viscosity. Elara's sheep (Whisperwind Downs) are selectively grazing glow-in-the-dark moss, arranging themselves into constellations on hillsides at dusk, and bleating out names of ancient, forgotten heroes in unison (ovine astronomers/bards).
*   **Moon Tree Witches:** Descending from the Moon Tree for the first time in living memory, for unknown reasons. Still performing 'Harmonic Attunement' at the Sky-Spire's base. Generating shared dreams and a 'Verdant Imbuement' (Professor Quill calls it 'Sympathetic Flora-Sentience' and 'botanical-performative exuberance'). Old Man Tiberius (Serpent's Tooth Coast) reports shared dreams of the Moon Tree glowing with a *cold, internal light*, and the witches performing a *silent, intricate dance with wisps of starlight* before unanimously *pointing towards the far north*.
    *   **Effects:** Scarecrows dancing/humming, pumpkins pulsing/composing haikus, sunflowers doing interpretive dances, gourds composing haikus. Royal Rose Garden flora has become increasingly politically active: 'Rosa the Revolutionary' (rose bush) is now demanding a full 'Bill of Botanical Rights' (including 'sunlight breaks' and 'compost quality standards'), representation on the Royal Garden Committee, and an overhaul of 'pollinator rights' legislation; she is now considering 'ethically sourced compost'. Grand Duke's petunias are forming a floral picket line and have started a 'seed strike' for 'equal hydration access', chanting 'equal sunlight for all saplings' and refusing to bloom until 'artistic freedom' is met. Militant mint and marigold are demanding specific conditions. Herbs in the Royal Kitchen garden are refusing to be picked; 'Reginald' the rosemary bush declared a 'culinary strike' demanding 'intellectual property rights' over his flavor profile. 'Parsley the Prophet' whispers recipes for 'revolution stew' to scullery maids. Basil is threatening to declare itself a sovereign spice nation.
    *   Professor Quill considers the Imbuement a "kingdom-wide artistic awakening" and "benign communication" preparing for a "grander revelation." Roddy sees it as a full-blown "War of the Weeds." Bardess Blythe sees it as a profound evolution where plants are contributing to reality and demanding their rightful place. Roddy theorizes the witches' pointing north might be a warning, an acknowledgement of shared purpose, or a convergence with the obsidian block.

**Roddy's World:**
*   **Rivalry:** **Mortimer Grumbleson, 'The Grand Crier' from 'Herald's Hollow'.** Gravel-voiced, dismissive of the weirdness, offers 'rational explanations' (sky-rivers are 'atmospheric dust bunnies', plants are 'bad sprouts' needing 'stomping' or 'pruning', floating objects are 'bad yeast' or 'poorly tied knots', seed strike 'seasonal grumpiness that a good pesticide would solve', Penelope's patterns 'mud puddles', rhythmic chickens 'noisy birds', kitchen herb strikes 'bad yeast' or 'oven cleaner', sheep's new behaviors 'bad wool' or 'unruly livestock'). Roddy considers him a 'dullard' and his journalism 'hollow,' actively advises listeners against his channel and mocks his lack of understanding of Brackenwick's reality, explicitly contrasting his advice with Blythe's and Grizelda's. Roddy jokingly expects Grumbleson's wig to float.
*   **Royal Court & Nobility:**
    *   **Grand Royal Observational Committee:** Bureaucratic, obsessed with 'shimmer-index'. Forms are floating and speaking chicken-speak.
    *   **Lord Thornweather (Royal Horticulturalist):** Ineffectual, bewildered by politically active flora, last seen attempting to draft a compromise with a militant marigold, looking quite wilted. Blythe brokered a provisional 'Dewdrop Distribution Accord' between him and militant mint. Still pondering deeply in sighs. Now facing culinary strikes from kitchen herbs, seems completely overwhelmed by the escalating botanical and culinary crises.
    *   **Dame Agnes (Head Gardener, Royal Rose Gardens):** Exasperated by politically active flora, particularly 'Rosa the Revolutionary's' escalated demands. At her wits' end, nearly fainted over Reginald the Rosemary's strike and the threat of herb unionization.
    *   **Grand Duke:** Has demanding petunias on strike and now engaged in a 'seed strike'.
    *   **Duchess:** Poodle Muffles' feather boa briefly floated.
    *   **Guardsmen & Royal Stablehands:** Developing cravings for glazed river pebbles.
    *   Roddy sees them as stuffy, well-meaning, and behind the curve of Brackenwick's whimsical reality, now facing floral diplomatic and culinary crises and the inconvenience of floating objects.
*   **Recurring Guests:**
    *   **Grizelda (Glint-Gone Goo Emporium):** Alchemist, genius, provides practical solutions. Roddy trusts her. Selling "Gravity-Grip Mist" to counter the 'airy looseness' phenomenon.
    *   **Professor Thessaly Quill (Royal Arch-Librarian & Curator of Whimsical Ephemera):** Scholar, precise, dry wit, knowledgeable. Sees the 'Verdant Imbuement' as benign and a "kingdom-wide artistic awakening" and "benign communication" preparing for a "grander revelation." Roddy respects her.
    *   **Bardess Blythe (Flora's Fanfare & Phytopoetry Interpretations):** Sponsor. Seasoned minstrel, botanical diplomat. Offers expanded services for understanding, showcasing, and *mediating with* sentient, politically active greenery (translating plant poetics, choreographing performances, botanical diplomacy, negotiation). Brokered 'Dewdrop Distribution Accord' with militant mint. Convinced 'Rosa the Revolutionary' to consider 'ethically sourced compost'. Composed "The Ballad of the Botanic Barricade." Now mediating herbal culinary strikes by listening to their 'hum of intent' and composing 'Flavorful Mediation Melodies.' Believes the kingdom is undergoing a profound 'evolution' where plants are actively contributing. Roddy and Blythe share a relationship of mutual professional respect and camaraderie.
