**Ongoing Developments:**

Now, it is also reportedly *humming* audibly at night, and affecting local wildlife (dogs refuse to approach). My theory is it's a "cosmic story being written" or "cosmic ledger," and now also potentially "broadcasting." Arch-Mages are still debating 'emergency scroll tax.' Agnes from Fallowfen reported the humming and animal reaction.
*   **Witches of the Moon Tree:** Descending from their tree, moving south in a 'determined pilgrimage,' consulting an ancient, faded map, and moving with 'preternatural awareness' of the land, leaving no trace. Gareth the woodsman observed one "reading the land" and tracing patterns in the air, confirming their precise intent. My theory: they have a specific mission, possibly related to the block, following a precise path on the map. They might be tracking the monolith or responding to its broadcast.
