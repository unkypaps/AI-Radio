PART ONE -- Wizz said my name again! Goodness gracious, I nearly dropped my measuring scoop. "Acknowledged Theron's letter to show appreciation for listener interaction." To think my simple musings might be appreciated by the Wizz himself... it makes an old miller feel like his thoughts are worth something beyond the dusty walls of the mill.

But then... "the third mill in Stonebrook Hollow." Stonebrook Hollow? My heart gave a funny little lurch, like a gear slipping. I was so sure, after last time, that *our* mill, here in Whispering Falls, was the "third mill" for which Elara and Barnaby's grand design was intended. Wizz said my question about the mill's health "echoed" their solution for the *third* mill. I even started picturing our old stones turning counter-clockwise, resonating with the moon.

Well. It seems I might have gotten ahead of myself. Or perhaps Stonebrook Hollow is the *first* of the "third mills" to get the treatment? It's a bit confusing, I won't lie. A small part of me feels a little deflated, like a sack of grain that's lost its loft. I was so ready for *our* mill to become that "resonant nexus."

Still, I'm absolutely eager to hear about the initial effects. If it works for Stonebrook Hollow, it proves the brilliance of Elara and Barnaby's wisdom. And if it proves true, well, then it's just a matter of time, isn't it? Our turn will come. I hope. It means the kingdom's pulse is truly being mended, mill by mill. I'm still proud that my question, my worry for our mill, was a part of the conversation, even if the implementation starts elsewhere. I suppose it's like a baker trying a new recipe in a different oven first. Better to learn the quirks before you try it in your own. I'll still tell Pip, but maybe I'll temper my enthusiasm just a touch this time.
