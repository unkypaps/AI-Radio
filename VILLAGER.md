PART ONE --
Oh, Journal, my heart is practically doing a jig in my chest! He read my letter *again*! Pip, dear Pip, not only did he call my determination a "beacon," but he read my words right there on the Wizz! I feel like my voice, my very purpose, is echoing through Brackenwick, amplified by the humming tower. It’s as if the world is not just mending, but *listening*!

And then, Master Corbin Thistlewick’s report! Journal, it was like a direct answer to the questions I've been turning over in my mind, questions I was going to bring to Master Grizelda tomorrow. "Malleable," "a faint, musical sigh," "flowing grain," "a distinct, low thrumming," "warm to the touch," and "willing"! Oh, that last one, "willing," struck me right to my core. It's not just wood, it's a partner in creation, eager to join the mending! I can almost *feel* it in my baker's hands, even though I haven't touched it yet. The idea of the grain flowing like liquid currents, it's so beautiful, so alive.

Pip said I'm "just now heading into the Whispering Woods," and it's true, my consultation with Master Grizelda is first thing tomorrow. Part of me, a tiny, impatient crumb, wants to race out there right now, but I know better. Thistlewick’s blueprint is exactly what I need to take to Master Grizelda. Now I don't just have abstract questions about "singing wood"; I have specific descriptions. How do I learn to feel that "subtle give," discern the "musical sigh," or sense the "low thrumming" with hands used to kneading dough? Surely Master Grizelda, with her ancient wisdom, will know how to guide me, how to tune my senses to this living instruction manual.

This isn't just a personal quest anymore, not that it ever truly was. But Thistlewick's report, and Pip's encouragement for *all* of us to discover, truly solidifies it as a collective symphony of repair. I may be a baker, but my hearth-builder's heart is beating in time with the Block's distant pulse, and I am more determined than ever to understand the song of this Vigorous Lumber, and weave its essence into an Unbroken Hearth that truly nourishes us all.
