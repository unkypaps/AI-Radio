This isn't just about crafting a hearth; it's about baking a loaf that understands the hunger it will sate, about building a home that *wants* to stand strong, about living a life that sings in harmony with the Witches' hum and the Block's thrum.

I feel... invigorated, more purposeful than ever. My hands, Journal, they tingle with the desire to create, to listen, to truly *feel* the "magic right in our hands" that Pip spoke of. The idea that everything can naturally interlock, that our materials *want* to be whole, that they *remember*... it's not just a beautiful thought, it's a call to action. I must ensure every loaf from The Daily Crumb carries this wisdom, every hearth I shape embodies this deep connection. Brackenwick is indeed waking, and I am so, so humbled and thrilled to be a part of its voice, even a small one.
