Oh, journal, my heart feels as if it might simply float away with joy! He read it! He read my letter again, *my exact words*! And this time, he called them a "melody of purpose"! Can you imagine? How will the Anchor re-educate these spaces, bringing them into a new, vibrant balance? I am so eager to begin.

Pip’s words about linking "ancient wisdom of the Witches' blueprints to the living, breathing reality of your garden" resonate so deeply with me.
