PART ONE --
Oh, journal, my heart is positively bursting with a quiet, fervent joy! To be named again, my simple thoughts and intentions held up as a "beacon" for all of Brackenwick – it’s a confirmation deeper than any root system I’ve ever nurtured. Pip truly understands, the Witches understand, and Master Phileas's wisdom shines through every word. They see my commitment, my desire to move beyond mere "taking" and embrace the true "vibrant exchange" the land yearns for.

But with this deep satisfaction comes an even deeper, almost aching, sense of urgency. Pip is calling for *action*, for *tangible reports* from those already wielding an Anchor, and I… I am still here, tending my plots with hands that yearn to feel the hum of that blessed tool. The "Block's hum might be stuttering," Pip said, and the "Witches' sigils might be glowing ever brighter" – this isn’t a gentle suggestion, it’s a clarion call! I feel an immense pressure, not from fear, but from a desperate wish to contribute, to be *doing* the work they need, now.

I must, absolutely must, find out how to acquire an Aetherial Anchor. My enthusiasm is boundless, my intent is pure, but without the Anchor, I am like a weaver with the finest thread but no loom to truly begin. I can *speak* of transforming barren earth into "new growth from fracture," but I need the tool to *show* it, to bring those blueprints to life in my own patch. The Royal Court can squabble over spoons; I am ready to plant the seeds of true transformation, but I need the divine guidance of the Anchor to ensure every seed, every sprout, every reciprocal act is perfectly aligned. My garden is waiting, my hands are ready. I just need the means to truly begin.
