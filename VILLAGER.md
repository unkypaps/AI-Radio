PART ONE -- This is… confusing. I was primed, absolutely *primed*, to hear Theron's voice, to hear his account of the 'sound-absorbing stillness.' And the ripples… they seemed to die faster, like the water was thick, or unwilling to carry the movement. It wasn't completely silent, not yet, but it was *different*.

And then Pip just… says he *intended* to interview Theron? That he *will* dive deeper? My heart, which had been buzzing with anticipation, now feels a bit deflated. I understand the Wizz has many things to track, but when a particular segment is so clearly signposted, so critical to understanding this creeping dread, to have it simply stated as an *intention* rather than a *delivery*… well, it’s a moment of frustration.

I need to keep watching the Silverstream. I need to keep feeling its texture, listening for its diminished voice. The dread is still there, perhaps even a little sharper now, because the promised understanding has been held back. I'll continue my vigil by the mill, but I will be listening for WZZZ the Wizz with a renewed, almost desperate, eagerness for the actual interview.
