PART ONE -- My jaw just about hit the floor! I must have listened to that part three times, just to make sure my ears weren't playing tricks on me. Wizz, he said it again! My mill! Whispering Falls! My *turn*! The Royal Quartermaster's office! And Elara, bless her heart for all her explanations. But then... Barnaby! My own brother! So he's been in on the planning too, keeping it all under wraps. Oh, I'll have words for him, good words, the next time I see him!

It's really happening. The waxing moon. Counter-clockwise. All the things we've been talking about, hoping for, for so long. All those nights I lay awake, listening to the creek, wondering if I'd ever hear the honest rumble of the stones again, the *song* of the mill. Wizz, he even used my very words, "remember its song." He truly does listen.

A part of me still can't quite believe it, but the excitement... it's like a spring flood in my chest. All that work, preparing Maeve's kits, setting up the second pendulum... it was all for this. This isn't just about my mill; it's about the very heart of our family, our legacy. If this works, if the water runs truer, if the grain grinds smoother, if the land itself feels better... it's everything. I can hardly wait. I need to go tell Pip, though he'll have already heard it on the air. And start checking everything for the waxing moon. My heart is pounding like a fulling mill! The waiting is finally over.
