Like a good sourdough starter, working its magic without a fanfare.

My determination to bake that special, sustaining loaf for Maeve and her sisters feels even more vital now. It's not just food; it's a tangible expression of this "quiet act of truth-keeping" that the Wizz spoke of. We all do our part, in our own way, to keep the true record alive, even if it's just with a warm loaf and an honest letter. My heart feels not just full, but resolute. The ovens are warm, and so is the spirit of Brackenwick, beneath all the noise.
