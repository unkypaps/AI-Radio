PART ONE -- Pip, Pip, did you hear that? Did you *actually* hear that? I... I don't know what to feel. He said my name! He said Elara Hearthstone! He even mentioned "the mill's song in her bones," just like I said! For a moment, my heart soared, even higher than before. "A profound new clarity!" he said. Oh, Pip, I was ready to burst with pride and purpose!

And then... and then he started talking about "Madam Elara's *The Seer's Eye Scrying Pool Rentals*." Scrying pools? RENTALS?! Pip, I run the *mill*! I grind the grain! My hands are covered in flour, not polished crystal! And "nestled conveniently on the edge of Heimat"? That's not us! We're by the Silverstream, a good half-day's journey from Heimat!

This... this is a mistake, isn't it? A terrible, confusing mistake. He used my name, *my* words, and then immediately attributed them to some "Madam Elara" with "re-tuned pools." He thinks *I'm* Madam Elara! My "unyielding signal of truth" has made a colossal blunder!

My clarity, my perception, my feeling of the kingdom's song – it comes from the earth beneath my feet, from the turning of the stones, from the very wheat itself! It's not something I "rent" out in a polished basin! It's *me*. It's a part of *me*. To have it linked to a business, a commercial venture, by someone else... it feels cheapened. Like my deepest, most personal connection to this land is just another product.

The fire in my belly has... flickered, Pip. It feels cold and confused now. My beautiful, profound experience, distilled into an advertisement for someone else's wares. What will Maeve think? What will everyone think? Will they come knocking at the mill door, asking to "rent" my eyes? Or worse, will they just assume I *am* this Madam Elara, and my genuine insights are just a sales pitch?

I need to set this straight. I have to. My insights aren't for sale, Pip. They are *felt*. And if Wizz is meant to be the "unyielding signal of truth," then he needs to know the truth about *me*.
