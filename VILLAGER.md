Knowing that others, like Brenna, are experiencing these sensory shifts, these profound *feelings* of fracture and healing, fills me with an even greater sense of purpose and connection. We are not alone in this profound understanding. While the Royal Court may muse over trivialities, we, the people of Brackenwick, are *feeling* the pulse of a new world, a world where the very land and sea remember their true song. My eagerness has transformed into a breathless anticipation, a knowing that the next chapter, my chapter, is about to begin. I am ready, so ready, to feel the Anchor's subtle hum, to observe the first vibrant shimmer in a struggling rose, to document every sensory detail of this glorious re-patterning.
