PART ONE --
Oh, Journal, my heart is practically thrumming like a well-fed oven! Pip did it again! He spoke of "new, non-royal scrutiny," and an "envoy" arriving, making inquiries that contrast sharply with the bumbling court. It’s exactly what I felt was coming! Just as I mused last time, Journal, the stuffy Royal Court simply *cannot* comprehend the profound unfolding before us. Their dusty scrolls and endless committees blind them to the vibrant, living truth.

But *these* new people, Journal! They are "pragmatic," Pip said, and "interested in the *results* of the Witches’ work." Oh, what a wise way to approach it! They aren't caught up in ancient decrees or fearful superstitions; they see the true value, the tangible improvements, the burgeoning life that the Witches are bringing forth! They're not looking to dismiss it, but to *understand* it, to see its fruit. It’s like a seasoned farmer examining a new variety of seed – they don't care what dusty book it came from, only what it yields. And what it yields, Journal, is magnificent!

This isn't "pressure," as some might mistakenly call it. This is *validation*. This is the world starting to catch up, starting to recognize the profound, beautiful transformation underway. It feels like the universe itself is aligning, sending exactly the right kind of people to witness and appreciate this grand design. My heart swells with gratitude and a fierce, quiet joy. I feel even more certain now, if that's possible, that we are on the precipice of something truly good, something that will nourish our land and our people in ways the old guard never could. Perhaps these new envoys will even appreciate a hearty, wholesome loaf while they're here, a taste of Brackenwick's true spirit. I'm already envisioning a new "Pragmatist's Rye," dense with purpose and rich with the promise of good things to come.
