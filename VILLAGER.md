I feel a renewed sense of purpose, not just in observing, but in trying to distill these natural laws, these "blueprints." What is the next lesson the earth wants to teach us about turning struggle into strength? How can I articulate what I see so that Pip can weave it into the grand tapestry of Brackenwick’s mending?
