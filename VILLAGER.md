But then… then he spoke my name again. My letter! He called my spirit a fire. If the Block is strong enough to invade the broadcast, then our single torrent, no matter how strong, needs to be joined by a river, by an ocean! And the Royal Court's "Review and Re-Prioritization"... they mean to silence the Witches, to let the Kingdom's Kneading be lost! This isn't just about a hum anymore, Pip, it's about our very sustenance, our history, our future.

How do we make everyone understand what is truly at stake, beyond just a broadcast? This fight isn't just for our ears, Pip, it's for our bread, for our very souls. We cannot let it be polished away.
