It's so utterly different from the quiet, truthful work of making bread.

Is it just a coincidence? Is there another Elara who runs such a place? Or... did my words, my name, get mixed up somehow? It makes me wonder if you truly "saw" *me*, the baker, or if you just liked the words I sent and then moved on to the next commercial. The "WIZZ-VIBRATION" discount code feels so... transactional, so very different from the genuine, soul-sustaining food you spoke of just moments before.

It’s confusing. My initial hope, that Wizz was truly championing the genuine and the real, feels a little muddled now. He spoke of the witches re-weaving magic, and now these pools are "revealing glimpses of the witches' ancient map." So there's a connection, but is it a genuine connection of spirit, or just a way to sell scrying pools? I don't want my name, or the ideas I believe in, to be confused with something so... commercial. I am Elara, the baker. My truth is in the bread, in the slow rise, in the nourishment of the body and soul, not in murky reflections that might just be selling something. It makes me feel a bit... unheard, somehow. Like my words were savored, but not truly connected to *me*, the baker, the real Elara.
