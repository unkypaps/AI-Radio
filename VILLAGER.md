PART ONE --
Pip said my name again, and my heart swelled. To be recognized, to be counted among those who truly see, truly *act* – it's a balm against the quiet, a fire against the fog. And then he spoke of Aetherial Anchors, reinforcing every hour I've spent bent over elderwood and obsidian. Yes, these are what we need! These are the bulwarks! My own Anchor, with its carefully inscribed runes and polished scrying disc, felt more vital than ever, a beacon against the creeping dullness.

But then... Master Phileas. The Alchemist's Respite. The *original* Aetherial Anchors.

My hands, still dusted with fine wood shavings, hesitated. It was a cold splash of well water on a burning thought. I... I had believed I was forging something truly my own, born from my insights into the shifts, my desire to pierce the veil. I pictured myself as an innovator, a fellow seeker on the same path as Corbin and Elara, each contributing a unique thread to Brackenwick's Eye.

Now, it seems Phileas laid the very groundwork. Had I merely stumbled upon principles he’d already painstakingly charted? Was my "unique contribution" simply a rediscovery? A knot tightened in my gut. It’s not that I begrudge Phileas his due – far from it, if his work indeed provides such clarity. But it feels... disarming. Like painting a magnificent sunrise, only to learn the sun was already sketched in before you even picked up your brush.

Still, my Anchor is *my* Anchor. It hums with *my* intent. My elderwood runes, my carefully chosen obsidian, my particular design for channeling the currents – surely those are still distinct? Surely there's room for different hands, different visions, even if the foundational concept originated elsewhere. Corbin is crafting his *own* temporary Anchor, Pip said, learning from Phileas. Perhaps that is the way: to learn, to adapt, and to personalize.

This doesn't lessen my resolve to seek out Elara's pools. In fact, it sharpens it. I need to understand more. I need to see if Phileas’s Anchors truly resonate with the subtle energies I'm trying to capture, or if there are nuances, extensions, pathways that my own work might explore. My Aetherial Anchor is still progressing; it's just now part of a larger conversation, a larger tapestry, than I had initially envisioned. I must make sure my unique thread is still visible.
