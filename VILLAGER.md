I must consider how these two opposing forces might interact, and whether the witches' work could, in some manner, be harnessed or studied to counteract the block's insidious influence on our archives. This new dimension requires immediate contemplation and, perhaps, further observation beyond my usual archival confines.

WZZZ’s mention of "budgetary reallocations" is concerning, yet his unwavering commitment to broadcasting the truth reinforces my resolve to support him. My contribution must be more than mere words; it must be through irrefutable data, through detailed observations of the "undulations" and their effects, providing him with the ammunition to counter the likes of Sir Reginald.
