PART ONE --
Oh, thank the stars! Thank the stars above Brackenwick! Pip sounds renewed, doesn’t he? And Master Phileas! It’s like a huge, heavy weight has been lifted from my chest. I was so worried for them, for the Wizz, for all of us. To hear Master Phileas’s voice again, and to hear that the Anchors are working, that people are actually *perceiving* things more clearly… it gives me such hope. The static is still there, yes, but it doesn't feel as... suffocating. There’s a *flicker*, Pip said, and I felt it too, a little spark in my own heart.

But then, the words he used… "a deliberate rhythm," "a directional impulse," "a command to mend something broken." That’s… that’s big, isn't it? It's not just listening anymore, it's *doing*. It's not just understanding what’s happening *to* us, but understanding what we’re being asked to *do*. And Master Phileas talked about "realigning scattered pieces" and "re-stitching" patterns of energy. It sounds like something only a very clever person, or someone with strong magic, could do. Me, I mend torn flour sacks and re-stitch the hem on my apron. My mending is much smaller scale.

It does make me wonder, though. If it’s a command, and it's about mending, then everyone must have a part to play, mustn’t they? Even those of us who can’t go wandering through ley lines with Aetherial Anchors strapped to our ears. My shop still needs me, more than ever now that folks are feeling a bit more hopeful and are starting to come out again for a scone or a loaf. I can't just up and leave The Daily Crumb to go seek out a "high-probability zone" and listen for ethereal chords.

But I *can* still listen to my customers. If those who *do* go to these zones are feeling "a profound sense of urgency" or a "strong compulsion to realign," then surely they'll talk about it. Maybe I can be the one who gathers those scattered pieces of information, the different "commands" people feel, and bring them together, like ingredients for a new recipe. It’s not quite a cartographer’s map, but perhaps it's a map of intentions, of what the Witches want us to *do*. My ears are still open, Pip. More open than ever. I'll listen for the instructions, even if they're coming secondhand. I have to believe there's a place for everyone in this mending.
