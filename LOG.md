**[2026-06-19 13:09 UTC -- NEWS]**

Alright, alright, settle down folks! You're tuned into WZZZ the Wizz, broadcasting live-ish from our tower just north of Heimat, coming at you on a signal nobody quite understands, least of all your pal, Roddy! And boy, do we have some news today that'll make your spell-books drop and your goblin-grog curdle. First up, the big one: up in the frozen, frost-bitten wastes of the far north, there's been a *thing*. A colossal obsidian block, we're talking a hundred yards tall and miles across, just… appeared! Out of nowhere! Covered in runes, folks, intricate carvings that no sage or scholar can decipher. Nobody knows where it came from, what it means, or if it's just really, really late for a party. My brain feels like a particularly muddled stew just thinking about it.

And if a giant, mysterious space-block wasn't enough to get your dragon-scales bristling, hold onto your hats! The witches. Yes, *those* witches, the ones up in the Moon Tree? They've started coming down. For the first time in living memory! Not just a quick grocery run to the village, mind you, but an actual descent, like they're migrating. Are they just tired of tree-top living? Are they looking for a good potluck? Or is this connected to that giant, silent monolith up north? Your guess is as good as mine, probably better, because frankly, I’m just trying to keep my head from spinning like a rogue turnip cart. Meanwhile, the Royal Council, bless their silk-lined tunics, are reportedly 'forming a committee.' A committee! While the fabric of reality feels like it's fraying at the seams!

You know, while some of the other 'news-bringers' – and I'm looking at you, Sir Lancelot 'The Loud' and your endless tirades about oat prices – are still going on about mundane matters, WZZZ is here for the *real* scoop! We've got giant mysterious blocks, descending witches, and a kingdom that's suddenly feeling a whole lot more whimsical than usual. What's next, talking squirrels? Don't even put it past this place. Stay tuned, because I've got a feeling things are just getting started. This is your pal, Roddy, keeping you informed, bewildered, and occasionally entertained. Back after these messages from the Goblin Grimoire Guild – 'We've got your spells, and we take gold *or* shiny pebbles!'

They walk like they're looking for something, or like something is looking for *them*."

Looking for something, or being looked for, huh? That's… not exactly comforting, Elara. Do you think it has anything to do with that giant, silent block of... *stuff* up north? The one that's got the Royal Council forming committees instead of, you know, actually doing anything useful?

"The block? Perhaps. Or perhaps it's simply the season changing in ways we don't understand, Roddy. There are old tales, hushed whispers from before written history, of when the Moon Tree itself 'wept starlight' and the world held its breath. The air feels like that now. Heavy. Full of unseen eyes. Whatever it is, it's not small. And it's not going to be ignored. You just keep broadcasting, Roddy. Someone has to tell the folk what their own eyes are seeing, since most of them are too busy gossiping about Lord Bellingham's new wig." Well, there you have it, folks! Straight from Elara Meadowsweet, who's seen more seasons than most of us have had hot meals. The witches aren't just stretching their legs, they're on a mission, or being pulled into one. And the air is heavy? Unseen eyes? My turnip cart brain is really working overtime now! Thanks so much, Elara, for shedding some light, or at least some intriguing shadows, on the situation. We'll be right back after this important message from 'Ye Olde Troll Bridge & Toll Booth Collective' – 'Don't cross us, or you'll pay the price!'
